## Instructions

### App Planning & Requirements Gathering
Clarify the core purpose and audience before building.

- **What is your app called?**
  Bluebird

- **What core problem does it solve, and for whom?**
  The Bluebird app aims to help users track and manage their mental wellness, providing a safe space for reflection and connection.

- **Can you describe the key workflow or the “main magic” moment for users?**
  The main magic moment is when users receive personalized recommendations for improving their mental wellness based on their reflections and interactions within the app.


### Essential Features
- **List the must-have features the app requires for a useful MVP.**
  Must-have features include mood tracking, personalized recommendations, and a community forum for support.

- **Which features are nice-to-have or for future versions?**
  Nice-to-have features include integrations with wearable devices, AI-powered chat support, and gamification elements.


### User Experience
- **What are the main user roles?**
  Main user roles include registered users and moderators/admins.

- **What should each user be able to do?**
  Registered users can track their mood, view recommendations, and interact with the community. Moderators can manage content and user reports.

- **Are there any access restrictions?**
  Access restrictions include moderation of user-generated content and reporting mechanisms for harmful or inappropriate content.


### Integrations & Notifications
- **Should the app integrate with external APIs or services?**
  The app should integrate with popular wearable devices and potentially with calendar apps for scheduling wellness activities.

- **For the MVP, do you want real integrations or simulated flows?**
  For the MVP, simulated integrations are acceptable, but real integrations should be implemented in future versions.

- **Should users receive notifications? If yes, how often and for what?**
  Users should receive daily/weekly summary notifications and alerts for community activity or important updates.


### Data, Security & Privacy
- **What core data or objects are stored?**
  Core data includes user profiles, mood tracking entries, and community interactions.

- **Should users have privacy settings or profiles?**
  Users should have control over their data visibility and be able to opt-out of certain data collection.

- **Any GDPR or compliance needs?**
  The app must comply with relevant mental health data regulations and GDPR.
